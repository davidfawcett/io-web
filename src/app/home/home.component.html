Our Inspiration

The Black Saturday bushfires was a series of bushfires that occured in 2009. With 173 fatalites, 414 non fatal injures and over 1.1 million acres affected. It is considered to be one of the all-time worst bushfires in Australian history.

Sandra was working to counsel youth affected by the bushfires and we were discussing how her work was going on a long drive back from Sydney.

We started discussing how difficult it was to track the progress of her clients over time. With my experience in IT, I found that surprising because there are a lot of excellent freely available tools to help with data collection.

But as I learned more and more about the problem I discovered that the existing solutions were too diffult and time-consuming. In general they are:

  • Tricky to set up
  • Very hard to deliever to clients
  • Complicated charting
  • Hard to secure

Then we started working on building a tool would do the job.

Why? Because we need data to ensure clients are travelling well. That data needs to be collated automatically so it can be accessed quickly. It needs to be secure but sharable.

Lastly, it cannot be a burden to clients. It has to be simple, fast and provide confidence and assurance.

How it works

Our web platform allows practitioners to create questionaries and prompts that are sent to the client's phone via a mobile app.

Clients then answer the question with results automatically collated as charts.

The questionnaires are fully customizable, allowing you to create a question in your own voice and according to the needs of the client. Building questions is very straightforward and you can save common questions as templates.

Questionaries can also be sheduled so they appear at regular intervals for clients.

The data generated by your clients is stored in a remote database that is HIPAA compliant and secure.

We also include several templates that measure the effectiveness of your sessions and gauge client mood.
